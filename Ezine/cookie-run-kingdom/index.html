Silent Salt Cookie Review (game)

In a fantasy realm of cookies - who stands at the top?

Oh my goodness - silent salt cookie sneak peeks from Silent Salt: Kingdom have just dropped recently and I cannot put into words how much I am excited for this update. For starters, this update will drop on the 24th of November along with 2 new cookies: Silent Salt and his underling.

To be honest, the Silent Salt cookie has got to be one of my favourite cookies by far. Especially gameplay-wise too, in which I'll tell you why: his ability summons his horse called Nox Black Salt and he dashes forward with his steed, slashing the moon in two exploding parts. It deals AOE damage to cookies and his base cooldown is 14 seconds!

People are saying that he could be the best beast yet to come. Some are also saying that he will knock Shadow Milk Cookies out of the current arena meta, but I doubt that.

Along with his awesome abilities, his design is also phenomenal and outstanding compared to the others. First, his main colour palette is a greyish purple colour which really fits his aesthetic. He often wears his knightly armour with a jagged sword, making him so mysterious. I love it! He has also never shown his face.

Anyways, that's generally my opinion on the new coming Silent Salt cookie and I hope I get him while drawing in the gacha. Until soon!

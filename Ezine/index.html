Our Purpose

This website was created for students of Mckernan as a place to share their stories, poems, and creative ideas with the school community. We want to give everyone a voice and inspire others to express themselves.

How To Submit

  1. Write your story, poem, or idea in any format you like.
  2. Submit it via the form below.
  3. Our team reviews submissions and features your work in the EZine for all to enjoy.
  4. Share your creation with friends and classmates to inspire others!
